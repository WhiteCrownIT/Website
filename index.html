FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical turnaround time?
Standard business valuations: 7-10 business days. Portfolio company quarterly valuations: 5-7 business days. M&A due diligence: 10-15 business days depending on complexity. Rush services available with 48-72 hour delivery for urgent needs at a premium.
How do you ensure data security and confidentiality?
We employ bank-grade 256-bit encryption for all data transmission, maintain ISO 27001-compliant data handling protocols, execute bilateral NDAs before engagement, use secure virtual data rooms for document exchange, and limit access strictly on a need-to-know basis. Your sensitive financial information never leaves our secure infrastructure.
What are your pricing models?
We offer flexible engagement models: (1) Fixed-fee per valuation report starting at $2,500, (2) Monthly retainer for ongoing portfolio valuations and advisory, (3) Hourly billing for ad-hoc consulting at $150-250/hour depending on seniority. Volume discounts available for multi-company engagements. All pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.
Are your valuations acceptable to auditors and regulators?
Absolutely. Our valuation reports fully comply with IFRS 13, IND AS 113, ASC 820 (US GAAP), and IRS guidelines for 409A valuations. As a Registered Valuer under Indian regulations and trained in Big 4 methodology, our reports are routinely accepted by Big 4 audit firms, SEC reviewers, SEBI, and international regulatory bodies.
Do you work with clients outside India?
Yes, over 70% of our client base is international‚Äîprimarily US, UK, EU, and Singapore-based PE/VC funds, audit firms, and corporates. We're experienced in cross-border transactions, multi-currency modeling, and jurisdiction-specific regulatory requirements. Time zone differences actually work in your favor with our 24/7 operational model.
What information do you need to get started?
For a business valuation: 3 years of historical financials, current cap table, management projections, details on recent funding rounds or transactions, and industry/competitive context. For due diligence: virtual data room access including financials, contracts, operational metrics, and legal documents. We provide a detailed information request list upon engagement.
